Transaction Guide
Stock IN Types:
  • Purchase: New goods purchased
  • Return: Customer returned goods
  • Adjustment: Stock corrections
  • Donation: Received donation
  • Transfer In: Transfer from another warehouse
Stock OUT Types:
  • Sale: Sold to customer
  • Damage: Damaged goods
  • Loss: Missing items
  • Usage: Internal usage
  • Return: Vendor return
  • Transfer Out: Transfer to another location
